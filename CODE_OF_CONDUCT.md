# 📜 Code of Conduct

This project follows a professional and respectful environment.

---

## 🧠 Our Standards

* Be respectful and constructive
* Encourage learning and collaboration
* Provide helpful and meaningful feedback

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or abusive language
* Misuse of project resources
* Unethical use of AI systems

---

## ⚠️ AI Ethics

Contributors must:

* Avoid harmful or biased implementations
* Respect privacy and data integrity
* Use AI responsibly and ethically

---

## 🛡 Enforcement

Maintainer reserves the right to remove contributions that violate these guidelines.
